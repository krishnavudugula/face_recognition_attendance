Faculty Registration

Faculty member registration is managed by administrators only. This ensures secure access and proper verification of staff credentials.

How It Works

  • Admin Registration: Department administrators register new faculty members with a unique ID and temporary password.
  • First Login: Faculty members log in with their credentials and capture their face biometric during first access.
  • Dashboard Access: Once face registration is complete, faculty can access the attendance portal.

If you are a faculty member, please contact your department administrator to be registered in the system.

Admin Contact: Contact your IT/HR department for assistance.
